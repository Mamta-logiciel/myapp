Micro & Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP)

MINISTRY OF MSME SCHEMES - Development Commissioner (DC-MSME) Schemes

  • ► Diagnostic study - maximum cost Rs.2.50 lakh.
  • ► Soft interventions - maximum cost of project Rs.25.00 lakh, with Government of India contribution of 75% (90% for special category States and for clusters with more than 50% women/micro/village/SC/ST units).
  • ► Hard interventions, i.e., setting up of CFCs – maximum eligible project cost of Rs.15.00 crore with Government of India contribution of 70% (90% for special category States and for clusters with more than 50% women/micro/village/SC/ST units).
